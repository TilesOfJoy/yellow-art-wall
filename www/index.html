The Yellow Art Wall is a community interest company that aims to regenerate local communities by creating welcoming spaces where people can meet and engage in art.

Spaces

Our first Yellow Art Wall Space is located in Ramsgate at The Wooden Box Cafe. We are actively seeking new spaces across the UK to host exhibitions and workshops. If you are a cafe, restaurant, bar or local community space with a spare wall and would like to increase your footfall by becoming a Yellow Art Wall space please get in touch at jayne@YellowArtWall.org .

Exhibitions

We offer free monthly art exhibitions in our spaces and our team of curators are always looking for fresh artistic talent to showcase their work on the Yellow Art Wall. If you are interested in exhibiting your work with us please apply via our Google form [opens in new tab].

Good Grief Workshops

At the heart of what we do are free to attend or subsidised community workshops which enable people to explore subjects such as loss, wellbeing, hope and joy in a safe space. Facilitated by a range of artists from ceramists to painters, our workshops give people a chance to develop new skills and make new friends. Everyone is invited and we reach out to our local community groups. If you are interested in facilitating a workshop we would love to hear from you. Email jayne@YellowArtWall.org .
